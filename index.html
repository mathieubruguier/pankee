With 5+ years of software engineering experience, I am an expert at developing fully fledged solutions. Be it front-end or back-end development, I always try to go the extra mile and spend a considerable amount of time perfecting the User Experience.

I like a lot of things

One of my main hobbies is gaming. I have been playing video games for as long as I can remember, thanks to my older brother who introduced me to the NES and Genesis. More than playing games, I love making them. It has now been my lifelong dream to make video games for a living.

When I am not gaming, I'm usually watching a movie or playing music. Some of my favorite movies are: The Shining, The Mist and the original Star Wars. I love playing the drums and rocking out to my favorite tunes just as much as relaxing while playing the piano.

I love animals, and the majority of my life has been shared with cats. Nowadays, I live with Kirby, my favorite fluffball. I love him, and I like to think that he loves me back.

Travelling has always been a big part of my life. Originally from France, I have visited many countries in the past ten years, and I don't intend to stop anytime soon. I plan to go next to Iceland, Australia and Thailand.
